# GESTURE ARCADE 🖐️

A game you play using your hands and your camera!

## HOW TO START
1. Open your terminal (the black box).
2. Type `python main.py` and press **Enter**.

## THE GAMES
1. **Gesture Pong**: Move your hand up and down to hit the ball.
2. **Fruit Ninja**: Use your finger like a knife to slice the fruit!
3. **Point Selfie**: See your face "skeleton" made of dots. Click "PHOTO" to save a picture.
4. **Flappy Bird**: Move your hand **DOWN** quickly to make the bird jump.
5. **Air Drums**: Move your hand **DOWN** fast to hit the colored blocks.

## HOW TO LEAVE
- Press the **ESC** key on your keyboard to go back to the menu.
- Press **ESC** again to close the game.

## WHERE ARE MY PHOTOS?
Check the folder called `captures`. Your selfies are saved there!
